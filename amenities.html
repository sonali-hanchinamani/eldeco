Amenities

At Eldeco Ballads Of Bliss, residents enjoy a curated selection of top-tier amenities designed to support a healthy, active, and community-centric lifestyle. From sports and recreation to wellness and relaxation, every corner is thoughtfully built to uplift modern living standards.

European Clubhouse

Elegant community space with indoor lounges, event hall, reading rooms, and more for relaxing and socializing.

Temperature Controlled Swimming Pools

Indoor pools usable year-round, ideal for lap training or weekend family fun in all seasons.

State-of-the-art Gymnasium

Fully equipped modern gym with cardio, strength, and functional fitness zones.

Jogging & Walking Track

Scenic, tree-lined track for your daily morning jogs or evening strolls.

Basketball & Tennis Courts

Full-size courts designed for serious players and casual weekend games alike.

Billiards & Indoor Games

Dedicated space for billiards, carrom, and board games for indoor entertainment.
